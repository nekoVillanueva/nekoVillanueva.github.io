About Me

Neko Villanueva is a Software Developer at WeCanCodeIT, a full-time 5-star rated coding bootcamp which teaches agile full-stack software development. He is currently enrolled in the Fall 2018 cohort with an expected graduation date of December 2018.

Prior to enrolling at WCCI, Neko spent over five years in a Customer Service/Retail environment before transitioning to work as a Direct Support Profesional. While working as a DSP Neko learned of the various assistance options clients with mental and physical disabilities had at their disposal due to advancements in technology. This discovery sparked his efforts to continue his education to advance in his life long passion for helping others and being at the forefront of the technological frontier. Neko decided to pursue his career as a software developer in hope of being able to create software and applications that make everyday struggles a lot less stressful.
